Welcome to SGS

The Student Guide System

Features

E-learning material

Having access to all the learning material you need at one place. Regularly updated by the instructors, in addition to extra material including exams, problem-sets and labs

Feedback

Students can give their feedback on courses and instructors, and can also recommend courses for others

Connectivity

There will be an easy communication way between students and instructors, students can request help while instructors might post an announcement

Vision

Our main goal is not only making it feasible for college students to reach to all the materials they need for any course in their college, but also we desire making them able to see other students feedback on the courses and instructors, besides being able to know which courses that might be interesting before taking it.
